Capturing Groups and Character Classes with Quantifiers

Capturing Groups and Character Classes with Quantifiers
Quantifiers can only attach to one character at a time, so the regular expression "abc+" would mean "a, followed by b, followed by c one or more times".
It would not mean "abc" one or more times.
However, quantifiers can also attach to Character Classes and Capturing Groups
Such as [abc]+ (a or b or c, one or more times) or (abc)+ (the group "abc", one or more times).
